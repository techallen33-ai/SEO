Why Work With Pete

The Tax Advantage Most Advisors Can't Offer

High net worth financial planning is inseparable from tax strategy. That's not an opinion — it's a reality that shows up in every significant financial decision: when to sell, how to withdraw, how to structure a business exit, whether to convert, how to give.

Before joining Edward Jones, I spent nine years as a Tax Advisor and Enrolled Agent — a federally licensed tax practitioner. That background shapes how I approach every client relationship.

I don't provide tax advice as a financial advisor. But I think about the tax dimension of every planning decision, and I work closely with your tax professionals to make sure your financial plan and your tax strategy are aligned — not working against each other.

For high net worth clients, that coordination can make a meaningful difference.
