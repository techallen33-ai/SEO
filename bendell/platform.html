Heather's Platform

Real issues. Real solutions. Real experience. This is what Heather Bendl will fight for in Tallahassee.

Our Commitment

A Platform Built on Real Life

Heather Bendl's positions aren't poll-tested talking points — they come from living, working, and raising a family in Volusia and Brevard County. Every issue below reflects a challenge she has personally encountered or witnessed in her community. She is running to fix what's broken, protect what's working, and build a stronger future for all of District 30.

Priority Issue #1

Education & Teacher Support

As a public school teacher herself, Heather knows exactly what Florida's education system needs — and what's holding it back. She believes every child deserves access to a quality education, and that starts with respecting and compensating the teachers who deliver it every day.

Heather will fight for competitive teacher salaries that make Florida a destination for great educators — not a revolving door. She will advocate for properly funded classrooms, safer schools, and less top-down bureaucratic interference in what teachers are able to teach. She supports strong science and STEM education, parental transparency, and school choice frameworks that empower families — not strip resources from public schools.

  • Competitive teacher pay & benefits
  • Increased classroom funding & resources
  • School safety improvements
  • Strong STEM & vocational education
  • Parental rights & curriculum transparency
  • Reducing unfunded mandates on local schools
Priority Issue #2

Family Court & CPS Reform

This issue is deeply personal to Heather. She has spent years fighting a family court system that she believes operates without proper oversight, due process, or accountability. She has seen firsthand how families can be torn apart by courts that prioritize conflict over children's well-being — and how CPS agencies can wield unchecked power over vulnerable families.

Heather will advocate for mandatory recording of all family court proceedings, stronger appellate oversight, independent review of CPS decisions, and enhanced due process protections for all parents. Children deserve a court system that puts their safety and stability first — not one that rewards attorneys who keep conflicts alive.

  • Mandatory transparency & recording in family courts
  • Independent CPS oversight and review boards
  • Stronger due process protections for parents
  • Guardian ad litem reform to truly protect children
  • Judicial accountability measures
  • Protecting parents from unconstitutional orders
Priority Issue #3

Environmental Protection & Coastal Stewardship

With a background in marine biology, Heather has a scientist's appreciation for Florida's extraordinary natural environment — and a deep understanding of the threats it faces. The Indian River Lagoon, Mosquito Lagoon, and the coastal ecosystems of Volusia and Brevard County are economic and ecological treasures that must be protected for future generations.

Heather supports science-based water quality protections, responsible development policies that safeguard wetlands and coastal habitats, and strong enforcement of environmental laws. Florida's natural beauty is not just a heritage — it's a major driver of tourism, fishing, and quality of life that must not be sacrificed for short-term profit.

  • Indian River Lagoon & Mosquito Lagoon restoration
  • Science-based water quality standards
  • Coastal habitat protection from overdevelopment
  • Clean water protections for Volusia & Brevard
  • Marine ecosystem conservation
  • Responsible growth management policies
Priority Issue #4

Property Insurance Relief

Florida's property insurance crisis is real, and it is devastating families across Volusia and Brevard County. Homeowners are seeing their premiums double and triple, or finding their policies canceled outright — often with no warning and no recourse. This is not just an inconvenience; it is a financial catastrophe for working families and retirees on fixed incomes.

Heather will work to hold insurance companies accountable, push for real regulatory reform that protects policyholders — not just corporate profits — and support policies that lower costs for homeowners while improving coverage. Floridians have paid into their insurance for years; they deserve the coverage they were promised.

  • Insurance company accountability & transparency
  • Meaningful regulatory reform for homeowners
  • Anti-fraud measures that actually lower costs
  • Protection against bad-faith claim denials
  • Support for Citizens Insurance accessibility
  • Hurricane mitigation incentive programs
Priority Issue #5

Civil Rights & Constitutional Protections

Heather has fought for her own constitutional rights — and she will fight for yours. The right to due process, the right to be heard in court, the right to raise your children, the right to keep and bear arms, the right to free speech — these are not political talking points. They are the foundation of American freedom.

Heather believes that government at every level — including family courts, CPS agencies, and state bureaucracies — must be held to constitutional standards. She will work to strengthen protections against government overreach, ensure equal treatment under the law, and restore Floridians' faith in a justice system that is supposed to serve everyone equally.

  • Due process protections at all government levels
  • Second Amendment rights preservation
  • Free speech & First Amendment protections
  • Parental rights in education & healthcare
  • Anti-overreach legislation limiting government power
  • Equal protection under the law for all Floridians
Priority Issue #6

Government Accountability & Transparency

As a whistleblower who has personally exposed corruption in the court system, Heather understands that accountability isn't just a campaign slogan — it's a daily requirement of good governance. She will push for sunshine laws to be enforced, public records to be readily accessible, and government officials to be held to the same standards as everyone else.

Heather believes that Floridians deserve to know how their tax dollars are being spent, how decisions affecting their lives are being made, and by whom. She will champion transparency measures that shine a light on backroom dealings and hold elected and appointed officials accountable to the people they serve.

  • Strengthening Florida's Sunshine Law enforcement
  • Public records access & government transparency
  • Independent oversight of state agencies
  • Term limits advocacy
  • Ethics reform for elected officials
  • Whistleblower protections
Priority Issue #7

Community Safety & Emergency Preparedness

Florida is hurricane country — and the past several years have made that abundantly clear to every resident of Volusia and Brevard County. When Hurricane Milton struck in 2024, Heather saw that Edgewater lacked a Community Emergency Response Team (CERT) and immediately got to work to create one. That is the kind of proactive leadership she will bring to Tallahassee.

Heather supports strong funding for local first responders, improved emergency management infrastructure, and community-level preparedness programs that don't rely on distant bureaucracies to show up in a crisis. She also supports law enforcement funding and public safety measures that keep our communities secure year-round.

  • Hurricane preparedness & emergency infrastructure
  • Community Emergency Response Teams (CERT)
  • First responder funding & support
  • Law enforcement resources & backing
  • Disaster recovery reform & accountability
  • Community resilience programs
Priority Issue #8

Healthcare Access & Affordability

Florida families — particularly those in Volusia and Brevard County — are struggling with rising healthcare costs, limited access to mental health services, and a system that often seems designed to serve insurance companies rather than patients. Heather believes that every Floridian deserves access to quality, affordable healthcare.

She supports policies that increase competition in the healthcare marketplace to bring costs down, protect individuals with pre-existing conditions, expand mental health services and crisis resources, and ensure that rural and underserved communities in our district have access to the care they need. She also strongly supports veterans' healthcare access — District 30 is home to many who served our nation.

  • Affordable healthcare & prescription drug costs
  • Mental health services expansion
  • Veterans' healthcare access
  • Pre-existing condition protections
  • Rural & underserved community healthcare
  • Medicaid reform that puts patients first
Priority Issue #9

Economic Growth & Small Business Support

Volusia and Brevard County are home to a vibrant mix of tourism, technology (the Space Coast), small businesses, and agriculture. Heather wants to foster an economic environment where every Floridian has the opportunity to build a better life — without bureaucratic red tape, excessive taxation, or regulations that favor large corporations over the small business owner on the corner.

She will work to reduce unnecessary regulatory burdens, support workforce development and vocational training tied to local industries, and advocate for tax policies that put money back in the hands of working families. She also supports the continued growth of the Space Coast economy and technology sector in Brevard County.

  • Reducing regulatory burdens on small businesses
  • Workforce development & vocational training
  • Supporting Space Coast & tech sector growth
  • Tourism & hospitality industry support
  • Tax relief for working families
  • Infrastructure investment for economic growth
