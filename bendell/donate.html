Campaign Finance Legal Disclosure

Contributions to Heather Bendl for State Representative, District 30 are governed by the Florida Election Campaign Financing Act (Chapter 106, Florida Statutes). Individual contributions are limited to $1,000 per election. The primary election (August 18, 2026) and the general election (November 3, 2026) are separate elections, each with their own $1,000 contribution limit. Contributions from corporations, limited liability companies, partnerships, labor organizations, and foreign nationals are strictly prohibited under Florida law. All contributions over $100 require the contributor's name, address, occupation, and employer to be reported to the Florida Division of Elections. Contributions are not tax-deductible as charitable contributions for federal or Florida state income tax purposes.
