Why Your Help Matters

Heather's campaign is built from the ground up by ordinary citizens who believe District 30 deserves real representation. No massive PAC funding. No corporate machine. Just neighbors helping neighbors — and that means every volunteer hour genuinely moves the needle.

The Republican primary on August 18, 2026 is a competitive race. The campaigns that win primaries are the ones with the most passionate, organized volunteers. That's where you come in.

🚪 Canvassing

Knock on doors in your neighborhood and talk to voters directly about Heather's campaign. The most effective form of voter outreach.

📞 Phone Banking

Call registered voters in District 30 from the comfort of your own home using our volunteer calling system.

📱 Digital Outreach

Share Heather's posts on Facebook, write letters to the editor, and help spread her message online.

🎪 Events & Rallies

Help set up, greet supporters, and staff Heather's campaign events across Volusia and Brevard County.

🪧 Sign Hosting

Place a yard sign or host a larger sign on your property to help build name recognition across the district.

✉️ Voter Mail

Help prepare and send campaign mailers and outreach materials to targeted voters in your area.

🗳️ Poll Watching

Serve as a trained poll watcher on Election Day to ensure the integrity of the vote in District 30.

💼 Office Support

Help with administrative tasks, data entry, and campaign operations at our campaign headquarters.
