What Sets Me Apart

A Financial Advisor Who Understands Taxes

Before joining Edward Jones, I spent nine years as a Tax Advisor and Enrolled Agent with H&R Block. That background shapes how I approach financial planning — because a strategy that ignores taxes is an incomplete strategy.

I didn't stumble into financial advising. I made a deliberate move because I kept seeing the same gap: people needed more than tax help. They needed a long-term plan. Now I help with both sides of that picture.
