About

Pete Arnold

From Long Island to Florida — Built on a Career of Helping People

I grew up on Long Island, New York, and made the move to Florida to plant roots in a community I now proudly call home. Today, my wife and I are based in Edgewater — and I wouldn't trade it.

My path to financial advising wasn't accidental. I spent nine years as a Tax Advisor and Enrolled Agent with H&R Block, sitting across the table from real people navigating real financial stress — small business owners buried in quarterly estimates, retirees surprised by their tax bill in April, families trying to make sense of it all. I was good at solving those problems. But I kept seeing the same gap: people needed more than tax help. They needed a long-term plan.

That's what brought me to Edward Jones. I wanted to be the person who could help from both sides — the planning and the tax picture — so that nothing falls through the cracks.

Who I Work With

I work primarily with two groups of people:

Pre-retirees and retirees who want to make smart decisions about Social Security timing, retirement income, and what their tax picture looks like once they stop working.

Small business owners who are great at running their business but haven't had time to build a real financial strategy around it.

Both groups tend to have one thing in common — they've worked hard and they don't want to make an avoidable mistake at a critical moment. That's where I come in.

Beyond the Office

When I'm not working with clients, you'll likely find me on a motorcycle or at a hockey rink. I'm a firm believer that balance matters — in life and in financial planning.

Faith and family are at the center of everything I do, and that shapes the way I show up for clients. I take this work seriously because I know the decisions we make together have real consequences for real people.
